Augustine C. Okoh
Physiotherapist

Biography

Augustine C. Okoh is a physiotherapist with special interest in geriatrics and neurology. He graduated with a Bachelor of Medical Rehabilitation/Physiotherapy from the University of Nigeria. He is currently an MSc in Gerontology (Distance Learning) student at the University of Southampton, the United Kingdom.

His research interest includes disciplinarity in elderly care and fall prevention among older people.

ERPAAN

Building capacity for research and care of the ageing, in preparation of the projected increase in the ageing population in the Sub-Saharan region of Africa.
