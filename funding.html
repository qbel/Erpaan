Funding Opportunities
Why You should Get Involved

This scholarship was established in 2018 by Emerging Researchers & Professionals in Ageing-African Network (ERPAAN) and funded by generous donations of several organsiations including MSc/PhD student of School of Rehabilitation Science, McMaster University, Hamilton in Ontario, Hands-on Preferred Medicare Services Limited in Nigeria and generous offer by some older adults located in Kinsgton Ontario, Canada. The aim of this scholarship is to stimulate problem identifying and solving approach for final year undergraduate student projects in Rehabilitation Programs (Occupational Therapy and Physical Therapy) and Social Work Programs in Nigeria. Eligibility competitors are final year students willing to conduct independent research among older adults.
