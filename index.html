About Research

Emerging Researchers & Professionals in Ageing- African Network is a research network created in 2017 with the purpose of bringing together early researchers and professionals in ageing in Africa. We conduct multi/transdisciplinary research examining key issues in ageing with the aim of informing ageing policies.

ERPAAN features the latest discoveries by scientists at top research institutions, Part of its objectives is to share research news directly with the public. Building capacity for research and care of the ageing, in preparation of the projected increase in the ageing population in the Sub-Saharan region of Africa.
