About ERPAAN

Emerging Researchers & Professionals in Ageing- African Network is a research network created in 2017 with the purpose of bringing together early researchers and professionals in ageing in Africa. We conduct multi/transdisciplinary research examining key issues in ageing with the aim of informing ageing policies.

To promote the development of ageing research and care of older adults in the sub-Saharan African region

Building capacity for research and care of the ageing, in preparation of the projected increase in the ageing population in the Sub-Saharan region of Africa.

Partners Engagement

The partners engagement refers to patients and public who are interested in partnering in some areas of research. Specifically, this is a pool of patient and public that want to participate in our research activities. We aimed to gain an idea on how to engage the public and the patient in developing patient/family-oriented research/clinical questions and answering such questions. We want partners to suggest areas of research that is of interest to them. We can have a round table to discuss the possibility and the necessary materials needed for it to happened.
